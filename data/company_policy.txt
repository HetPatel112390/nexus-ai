# ACME Corp (India) - Employee HR Handbook & Policies 2024

## 1. Working Hours & Attendance
- Standard working hours are from 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM IST, Monday to Friday.
- A mandatory lunch break is provided from 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM.
- Employees must log their attendance via the HR portal before 9:45 AM. Late check-ins beyond 3 times a month will result in a half-day deduction.

## 2. Leave Policy (PTO)
- Casual Leave (CL): 12 days per year (pro-rated based on joining date).
- Sick Leave (SL): 12 days per year. A medical certificate is required for continuous sick leave exceeding 3 days.
- Earned Leave (EL): 15 days per year, which can be carried forward to the next calendar year (maximum 30 days).
- Maternity Leave: 26 weeks as per the Maternity Benefit Act.
- Paternity Leave: 5 working days to be availed within 2 months of childbirth.

## 3. Payroll and Provident Fund (PF)
- Salaries are credited on the 1st of every month.
- Employees Provident Fund (EPF): 12% of the basic salary is deducted as employee contribution, with an equal matching contribution from the employer.
- Diwali Bonus: An annual performance-linked bonus is disbursed typically one week before the Diwali holiday, subject to company profitability and individual performance rating.

## 4. Public Holidays (2024 Schedule)
The company observes 10 mandatory public holidays:
- Republic Day (Jan 26)
- Holi
- Independence Day (Aug 15)
- Gandhi Jayanti (Oct 2)
- Diwali
- Christmas (Dec 25)
...plus 4 optional restricted holidays.

## 5. Resignation and Notice Period
- The standard notice period for all confirmed employees is 60 days.
- Employees on probation have a notice period of 15 days.
- The company reserves the right to buy out the notice period at its discretion.
